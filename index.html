Eco-Friendly Practices

  • Switch to energy-efficient appliances Use appliances with ENERGY STAR ratings for better efficiency.
  • Use renewable energy sources Consider solar panels or wind turbines for green energy.
  • Unplug devices when not in use Avoid phantom energy waste by unplugging idle devices.
